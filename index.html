Catan

Catan is a game that has an unqiue combination of luck and skill. The objective of the game is relatively straight forward. The object of the game is straight forward enough, be the first to earn 10 Victory points. It is played on a board made up of hexagonal resource tile and players compete to expand their territory and harvest said tiles. There is bargaining and deception . . . TBC

Turns are played in the following order:

  1. Trade cards or play Knight card
  2. Roll dice
    • If 7: Move robber to new tile and steal 1 resource from town adjacent to robber
    • Else: Players collect appropriate resources
  3. Trade cards or play Development card
    • Trades can be made to the 'bank' at 4:1
      • Trades can be made to ports at 3:1
    • Follow Development card instructions
  4. Build Phase
  5. Pass the dice
A simple game with endless possibilities.
